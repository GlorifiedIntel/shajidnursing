Diploma Programs

The Diploma Programs at Shajid Royal College of Nursing and Midwifery are carefully structured to prepare students for foundational and specialized careers in nursing and midwifery. These programs provide practical, hands-on training combined with essential theoretical knowledge, enabling students to deliver safe, ethical, and compassionate care across diverse healthcare settings.

Whether pursuing General Nursing, Basic Midwifery, or Post-Basic Specializations, students are guided by experienced faculty and exposed to real-world clinical environments that foster professional competence and confidence. The curriculum aligns with national standards set by regulatory bodies such as the Nursing and Midwifery Council, ensuring graduates are eligible for professional licensure and ready to contribute meaningfully to the health sector.

At Shajid Royal College, we are committed to raising a generation of skilled, service-oriented healthcare providers who uphold the dignity of life and are equipped to address the growing needs of communities. The diploma programs offer a strong platform for immediate employment or future academic progression in the nursing and midwifery profession.
