Frequently Asked Questions

To provide quick, clear answers to the most common questions prospective students, parents, and other stakeholders may have about Shajid College of Nursing. The FAQ will reduce repetitive inquiries, improve user experience on the website, and assist in guiding students through the admission and academic processes.

How can I apply to the college?

Are there hostel accommodations for students?

Yes, the college provides on-campus hostel facilities for both male and female students, subject to availability.

What is the cost of tuition and other fees?

Tuition and fees vary by program and academic session. A detailed breakdown is provided on the college's website or at the admissions office.

Do students go on clinical postings?

Yes, all students must undergo supervised clinical experiences at approved hospitals and health centres as part of their training.

Can I work while studying?

Due to the demanding nature of the programs, full-time employment is not recommended. However, part-time engagements may be possible with proper time management.

Is there financial aid or scholarship available?

Some scholarships and sponsorships may be available through government initiatives or partner organizations. Students are encouraged to inquire at the bursary or student affairs office.
