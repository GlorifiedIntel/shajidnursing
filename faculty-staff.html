Biology Department

The Biology Department at Shajid College of Nursing and Midwifery serves as a vital foundation for understanding the science of life and health. Our department is dedicated to providing students with a strong grasp of biological principles that are essential for careers in nursing, midwifery, and other healthcare professions. Through engaging lectures, hands-on laboratory experiences, and collaborative research opportunities, students explore key areas such as human anatomy and physiology, microbiology, genetics, and biochemistry. The curriculum is tailored to support the practical and academic needs of future healthcare providers, emphasizing the biological processes that influence patient care and public health.

Our faculty are passionate educators and mentors who strive to inspire curiosity, critical thinking, and scientific integrity. By integrating biology with clinical relevance, the department plays a crucial role in preparing our students to become compassionate, knowledgeable, and skilled professionals in the ever-evolving field of healthcare.

Chemistry Department

The Chemistry Department at Shajid College of Nursing and Midwifery provides a strong scientific foundation essential for understanding the biochemical and physiological processes that underpin nursing and midwifery practice. Our curriculum is carefully designed to integrate core chemical principles with real-world healthcare applications, enabling students to grasp the role of chemistry in drug interactions, diagnostics, and the human body's response to treatment.

Through hands-on laboratory experiences, interactive lectures, and collaborative projects, students develop analytical thinking, scientific reasoning, and problem-solving skills critical to modern clinical environments. Our faculty are dedicated to guiding future nurses and midwives in understanding how chemistry directly impacts patient care, from fluid balance and medication safety to infection control. The department is committed to academic excellence and supports students with innovative teaching methods, research opportunities, and a nurturing learning environment.
