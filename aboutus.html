About Us

At Shajid College of Nursing and Midwifery, we are committed to shaping compassionate, skilled, and knowledgeable healthcare professionals who are prepared to meet the evolving needs of the healthcare industry. Established with a vision to empower the next generation of nurses and midwives, our college stands as a beacon of excellence in nursing and midwifery education.

We offer a range of accredited programs designed to combine rigorous academic training with practical clinical experience.
