How to Apply to
Shajid Royal College of Nursing

Follow these clear steps to successfully apply to Shajid Royal College of Nursing and Midwifery. From account creation to resumption, we've got you covered every step of the way.

Start Application

STEP 1

Visit the Website

Go to the official college website Home Page and click on “Apply Now” or here or on "Start Application" to begin the process.

STEP 2

Create an Account

Sign up using your fullname, email, and phone number. Confirm your account via email or OTP.

STEP 3

Pay the Application Fee

Use Paystack or your Debit Card to pay the required application fee and unlock the full form.

STEP 4

Fill the Application Form

Provide personal details, academic history, UTME info, and upload your recent passport photo.

STEP 5

Submit & Download PDF

Review all details, then submit your application and download the acknowledgement slip.

STEP 6

Check Screening Information

Watch for updates on screening date, time, venue, and required documents.

STEP 7

Attend POST-UTME Screening

Appear in person with credentials: O’Level results, JAMB Score Slip, payment receipt, ID, and photo.

STEP 8

Wait for Admission List

Login regularly or check your email to know when your admission status is released.

STEP 9

Accept Admission Offer

Accept your admission online and pay the acceptance fee to secure your spot.

STEP 10

Clearance & Resumption

Report for clearance and resume school. Receive your matric number and begin your studies!
