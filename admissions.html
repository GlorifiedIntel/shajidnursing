How to Apply

1

Submit Application

Please ensure your application is submitted before the specified deadlines to be considered for admission. Early submissions are encouraged to facilitate timely review. For detailed information on application periods and deadlines, kindly refer to the official college admissions calendar.

2

Send Documents

Please submit all required supporting documents by the designated deadlines to complete your application. Timely delivery of transcripts, recommendation letters, and other materials is essential for your application to be processed without delay. Refer to the admissions calendar for specific submission dates.

3

Interview Process

Applicants may be invited to participate in an interview as part of the admissions review. Interviews will be scheduled according to the timeline outlined in the admissions calendar. Please ensure your availability during the designated interview periods to facilitate a smooth evaluation process.

4

Receive Decision

Admission decisions will be communicated according to the dates specified in the college admissions calendar. Please check your email and applicant portal regularly for updates. We appreciate your patience throughout the review process and look forward to informing you of the outcome.

Admission Requirements

Academic Records

Official academic transcripts/results from all previously attended secondary and/or post-secondary institutions must be submitted. Transcripts/Results should reflect consistent academic performance and meet the minimum GPA or the requirements as outlined by the college. International students may need to provide certified translations and credential evaluations.

Recommendation Letters

Applicants are required to submit one or more letters of recommendation from teachers, counselors, or other academic professionals who can speak to their qualifications, character, and potential for success in a college environment. Letters should be submitted directly by the recommenders through the designated application portal by the stated deadlines.

Personal Statement

A well-written personal statement is required as part of the application. This essay provides applicants with the opportunity to highlight their academic interests, personal experiences, goals, and reasons for applying to the institution.

Standardized Tests

Applicants may be required to submit official standardized test scores, such as JAMB, depending on the institution's policies.
