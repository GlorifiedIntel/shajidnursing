Applying for Admission

Click on "Apply Now!" above or "Start Your Application" to your left. You will be taken to the application page, fill the application form and submit

Copy Application Number and Invoice Number

Make sure to copy your application number and invoice number for future reference. Proceed to the bank to make Payment or Pay with your Debit Card

Continue Registration

After successful Payment, click on "Registration", Follow the prompts to complete your registration. Make sure to provide all necessary documents and information.

To Continue Registration, enter your Invoice Number and choose a password. Make sure to choose a strong password that you can remember.
